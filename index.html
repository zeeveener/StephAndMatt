Wedding Details

When and where is the wedding?

Join us in celebrating the marriage of Steph and Matt on September 13, 2025 at the Abel Farm.

The address is 253001 Range Road 245, Alberta

Google Maps has the best directions, so we suggest using that over Apple Maps which does not have good directions.

Range Road 245 is gravel so please plan accordingly.

RSVP Deadline?

Please RSVP no later than May 1, 2024

If you are unable to RSVP before that date, we may not be able to accommodate you at our wedding.

Will this be an indoors or outdoors event?

The ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception will all be held outdoors.

What time does it all start?

The ceremony begins at exactly 4pm. Please ensure you arrive with enough time to park, meet & greet, and find your seats.

What is the dress code?

We would love if everyone could dress up in Semi-Formal wear. Please no jeans.

Please keep the outdoors nature of the wedding in mind when dressing for the occasion.

Parking?

While there is a fair amount of space for parking, please consider carpooling to reduce the number of vehicles

We will be arranging for a bus to take people from the hotel in Strathmore to the ceremony. The bus will be taking guests back to Strathmore periodically throughout the evening.

Alternatively, the Wheatland Taxi is also available at your own cost.

Sleeping arrangements?

We have an arrangement with the Travelodge in Strathmore.

Although we were unable to block rooms for the wedding, they will provide you with a discount if you mention the Abel-Beemer Wedding

Due to us being unable to block rooms, please ensure you book your room ASAP!

Is there a gift registry?

There is no registry, and having everyone present to celebrate our big day is your gift to us!

Please do not bring gifts.
